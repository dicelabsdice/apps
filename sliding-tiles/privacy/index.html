Sliding Tiles Puzzle

This Privacy Policy explains how Sliding Tiles Puzzle handles information on Android and Windows. The game is designed to store most gameplay data locally on your device, while some third-party services may process limited data for purchases, notifications, website links, analytics, or advertising.

1. Summary

Sliding Tiles Puzzle does not require you to create an account. Your progress, settings, generated boards, personalized boards, favorites, recent levels, achievements, and similar gameplay data are generally stored locally on your device.

Depending on platform and features used, the app may also rely on third-party services such as:

  • Google Play Billing for Android premium purchases.
  • Google AdMob for ads in the Android free version.
  • Microsoft Store for Windows premium purchases, once enabled.
  • Platform notification systems for reminder notifications.
  • External web links if you open the Dice Labs website or related pages.

2. What the app itself handles

Stored locally

Level progress, best times, puzzle states, theme selection, audio settings, language, achievements, favorites, recent levels, generated levels, and personalized levels.

Not requested directly by us

We do not ask you to create a username, password, or in-app profile, and we do not directly request payment card numbers inside the app.

Internet use

The app may access the internet for ads, store purchases, opening external links, and any platform services required by those features.

3. Android-specific information

Ads in the free version

The Android free version may display banner ads and interstitial ads provided by Google AdMob. Ad providers may process data such as device identifiers, IP address, approximate location, language, app interaction data, or diagnostic information as permitted by their policies and applicable law.

In regions where required, a consent form or privacy options flow may be shown before requesting personalized advertising.

Premium purchases

Premium purchases on Android are processed through Google Play. We do not receive your full payment card number or banking credentials. Google may process billing, fraud prevention, tax, and transaction-related information according to its own terms and privacy practices.

Notifications

If you enable reminder notifications, the app may schedule local notifications on your device. These reminders are intended to encourage return play and are managed through platform notification systems.

4. Windows-specific information

No advertising at this time

The Windows version currently does not include in-app advertisements.

Premium purchases

If and when premium purchasing is enabled in the Windows version, purchases are expected to be handled through the Microsoft Store or related Microsoft commerce services. In that case, Microsoft may process transaction and account-related information under its own policies.

Local storage

As on Android, gameplay progress and editor-related data are generally stored locally on the Windows device where the app is used.

5. Third-party services and links

The app may rely on, link to, or interact with third-party services. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies, not this page alone.

  • Google AdMob / Google advertising services
  • Google Play Billing / Google Play Store
  • Microsoft Store and related Microsoft commerce services
  • GitHub Pages or website hosting used for Dice Labs pages
  • Any browser you use when opening external links from inside the app

If you tap links such as the Dice Labs website or privacy policy pages, your browser or the target site may collect information according to its own configuration and legal obligations.

6. Data retention

Local gameplay data usually remains on your device until you delete it, uninstall the app, clear the app's storage, or overwrite it by continuing to play or edit content.

Store providers and ad providers may keep certain records for legal, financial, fraud prevention, security, auditing, or compliance purposes under their own retention rules.

7. Your choices

  • You can uninstall the app to remove locally stored app data from the device, subject to how the operating system manages app files.
  • You can disable notifications from within the app or from your device settings.
  • You can choose whether or not to buy premium content.
  • Where applicable, you may be able to manage ad consent choices through Google-provided privacy options.
  • You can avoid opening external links if you do not want to leave the app for external websites.

8. Children

Sliding Tiles Puzzle is a puzzle game intended for a general audience. If you plan to publish the app in a child-directed category or under special family-oriented programs, additional compliance requirements may apply and this policy may need to be updated accordingly.

9. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in the app, legal requirements, third-party services, monetization, or platform-specific behavior. The date at the top of this page will be updated when meaningful changes are made.
